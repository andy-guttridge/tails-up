About Tails-Up

Tails-up is a small cat rescue centre based in Dorset. We have been dedicated to to finding homes for cats in the local area since 2000. We have two centres, one in Wimborne Minster and one in Dorchester.

We have a dedicated team of volunteers, and rely on donations to keep our doors open. If you would like to get involved, take a look at our Facebook page for news of fundraising events and volunteering opportunities.

  • Minimal adoption fee
